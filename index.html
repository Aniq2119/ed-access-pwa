ACLS Cardiac Arrest

  • Start CPR: Push hard (at least 2 inches) and fast (100-120/min). Minimize interruptions.
  • Attach Monitor/Defibrillator: Check rhythm.
  • Shockable Rhythm (VF/pVT):
    • Give 1 shock.
    • Resume CPR immediately for 2 mins.
    • Give Epinephrine 1 mg every 3-5 mins.
    • Consider Amiodarone 300 mg bolus, then 150 mg.
  • Non-Shockable (Asystole/PEA):
    • Resume CPR immediately for 2 mins.
    • Give Epinephrine 1 mg ASAP and every 3-5 mins.
    • Treat reversible causes (Hs and Ts).
